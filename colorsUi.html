13C.01.02
Working with colors and animations

In this assignment I experimented with color palettes and combinations that would look good together by following good design conventions.
During this assignment I have also learned how to use MotionOne which is reflected throughout the solution website. In addition, I have also learned how to apply a dark theme taking into consideration the user preferences.

Details

For dark theme color scheme I have chosen darker shades of the accent color I was already using in the base palette for the solution. This combination of colors is meant to be less of a strain on the eyes while still allowing an easy reading experience.

I implemented a scroll animation based on what I learned from the lectures. While scrolling, articles contained in the sections appear and slightly slide in from the left side. I chose to add this animation to make the webpage more dynamic and fun.
